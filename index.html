Our mission is simple but profound

 Our position and focus

At the heart of our foundation lies a deep commitment to those facing unique hurdles, including individuals with disabilities, seniors navigating the complexities of aging, and those grappling with financial hardships that impact their ability to meet essential needs like bills.

Supporting disabled individuals

For our disabled fellow citizens, we provide a lifeline of support, advocating for accessibility, healthcare, and opportunities that foster independence. Our programs aim to break down barriers, creating a more inclusive society where every individual has the chance to thrive.

Care for the young and Aged Population

As our population increases, so does our responsibility to ensure that everyone enjoys a life of dignity and comfort. The Americannativeheart Foundation is dedicated to creating environments that promote the well-being of our elderly and juvenile community members, offering assistance with healthcare, companionship, and everyday challenges.

$ Easing Financial Burdens

We understand the impact of financial strain on individuals and families. Through our initiatives, we strive to alleviate the burden of bills, offering assistance that provides a sense of relief and stability during difficult times.

Key statistics and impact highlight

Financial assistance

Assisted 1,200+ families facing financial hardships, preventing eviction and utility shut-offs. Distributed emergency funds, covering essential bills for 800+ individuals in crisis.

Support Programs

Engaged 500+ seniors in social activities, reducing isolation and promoting mental well-being. Provided home assistance to 200+ elderly individuals, ensuring a safe and comfortable living environment.

Disability Advocacy

Advocated for improved accessibility in 20 public spaces, enhancing inclusivity for people with disabilities. Conducted awareness campaigns reaching 15,000+ community members.

Educational Initiatives

Awarded scholarships to 50+ students with disabilities, fostering educational aspirations. Conducted workshops reaching 1,000+ participants on disability awareness and inclusion.

Community Outreach

Collaborated with 30+ local businesses for inclusive hiring practices. Engaged 100+ volunteers in community outreach, amplifying our impact.
