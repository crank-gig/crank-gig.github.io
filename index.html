This piece delves into more of quite riveting features of the leading mobile device which might have been overlooked on, or never thought of; iPhone to be precise.
This article has a few laid out.

Share location

Want to know how to quickly share your location when you are meeting with other iphone-using friends?
In the Messages app. Open the message thread, type "I'm at" into the interface and tap the space key. Then tap the Current Location (you may have to allow permission), and the app will send your location.

Notes Scanner

If you need to quickly and easily scan and send a document, your iphone can do the job.

  1. Go to your Notes app. Open a new note, tap the Camera icon on the toolbar at the bottom, and select Scan Documents
  2. Place your document on a flat, well-lit surface, and take a photo.
  3. Tap keep scan > save to save the document. If you want to share it, tap the Share icon at the top-right corner and select how you'd like to send the document.

Measure app

If you never seem to have a tape measure when you need one, the Measure app on your iphone, which uses augmented reality along with the camera on your device to record measurements, is incredibly useful.

  1. Open the Measure app and scan an item with your phone
  2. When a circle appears, place it at your starting point and press the "+".
  3. Next, move to your endpoint and press "+" once more.
  4. The screen will display your item's dimensions

iMessage full-screen effects

Why send a boring text? Add a full-screen effect to animate your text courtesy of iPhone's iMessage function.

  1. Open Messages and tap the compose button to write a new message or go to an existing conversation.
  2. Type your message.
  3. Touch and hold the Send button, then the Screen.
  4. Swipe left to choose a full-screen effect (echo, spotlight, etc.).
  5. Tap the Send button.
To replay a message effect that you receive, tap the Replay button under the text. If you don't see it, try checking your Reduce Motion settings.

Location Tracker

Your iPhone has tracked every location you've been at since you activated it.
To see how creepy your iPhone is, go to settings, privacy, location services, system services, and check out the frequent locations option. It can be quite eye opening to see a complete history of everywhere you've been.

No Power Button Performance

Your iPhone can work without a power button, if your power button breaks. But before that, Go to settings, general, accessibility, and turn on AssisstiveTouch.
This setting will project a small icon onto the screen that'll allows you to lock your phone without the need of a physical button. You can also turn off your phone using this. Your phone also turns on automatically when plugged in.

Spirit Level

An iPhone has levelling tool capacity, just as the case of a spirit level tool to get those perfectly straight edges. Your iPhone's compass has a hidden level that you can activate by swiping to the left.

Siri's Trangeder

Popular iPhone assistant's gender can be changed. Move to the settings, general, Siri, and scroll down to "voice gender". You can have your male Siri

Spotlight search

The spotlight search is really useful. Just swipe down on your homescreen to access it, and type whatever you need. It's more efficient to search for contacts or a certain song than it is to open up individual apps and scroll throught a hundred names or title.

Pal-specific Custom Vibration

An iPhone can give each of your friends a custom vibration notification for text messages.

  1. Go to contacts
  2. find someone, and hit edit in the top right corner
  3. Then go down to Vibration, tap default, and scroll down to Create New Vibration
There you can craft a unique buzz for each of your coolest and worrisome friends. Coolest in most cases.

aero tracker

An iPhone can literally tell if any planes are flying above you.
Just ask Siri "planes overhead?" and Siri gives you a little table with all of the planes in the air roughly above your current location. One you cannot visibly detect might be flying overhead.

Mistake Eraser

Mistakes can be deleted with a simply shake. Make a typo? Apply the wrong filter to a Photo?
Just shake your phone, it'll erase the last thing you did.

I bet those were intriguing to know.
Try something more cool by exploring this profound knowledge and also don't forget to tell a buddy of yours how cool you are with your device. Have a great user experience.
